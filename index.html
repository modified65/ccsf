Spring 2014 Courses

MMSP135B - Web Development for Designers Level ll

Thursdays, 6pm-9:50pm, Mission Campus 206

This hands-on course is a continuation of MMSP 135A, Web Design for Designers Level l (formerly Web Design with Dreamweaver), focusing on front-end production techniques for creating responsive websites, integrating interactive widgets, and developing custom themes for content management systems like WordPress. Students will spend the last third of the course working on a personal web project.

Feel free to email me at chris.clark@mail.ccsf.edu with any questions regarding this course.
