Spring 2016 Courses

VMD 142 - Web Production lll

Wednesdays, 6pm-9:50pm, Mission Campus 473

This hands-on course is a continuation of VMD 141 and focuses on front-end production techniques and best practices for creating mobile-friendly responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Exercises will cover leveraging front-end frameworks and libraries for rapid prototyping and custom theming for content management systems like WordPress. Students will spend the first half of the semester completing a series of exercises and the remainder of the course applying the technologies learned to a personal web project.

Feel free to email me at chris.clark@mail.ccsf.edu with any questions regarding this course.
